About Me

I am currently studying for a Ph. D. degree in University Of Science And Technology Of China (USTC). My supervisor is Prof. Yongdong Zhang. I am also a Junior Researcher at Computer Vision and Multimedia Lab of JD AI Research working with Dr. Wu Liu and Dr. Tao Mei. My research interests include multimedia computing, computer vision, and their applications in retail.
